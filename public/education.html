A simple 4-step ritual.

Creating moments of connection with yourself doesn't require complexity. Follow this gentle framework to build a practice that feels natural and sustainable.

01

Set the mood

Begin by creating a space where you feel completely at ease. Lock the door, dim the lights, or light a candle. Privacy and comfort are the foundation—this is your time, free from interruption or judgment.

02

Check in with your body

Take a few deep breaths and notice how you feel. There's no pressure to feel any particular way. Simply observe sensations, tension, or ease. This awareness helps you stay present and connected throughout.

03

Choose one intention

Rather than overwhelming yourself with expectations, select a single focus for this session. It might be exploring pleasure, releasing stress, or simply being curious. One intention is enough.

04

Close gently

Aftercare matters. Take a moment to ground yourself—drink water, stretch, or journal about your experience. Acknowledge what you've given yourself: time, attention, and care. This closing ritual honors the practice.
